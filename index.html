WHAI IS LED?

LED stands for Light Emitting Diode. These diodes are tiny little bulbs that you might have seen on electronic devices. LED displays make use of a huge collection of light-emitting diodes on a flat panel. These diodes are either red, blue, or green. Each of these diodes is capable of emitting light of different brightness. LED display is a flat panel display that uses an array of light-emitting diodes as pixels for a video display.

Why should we use LED Screens?

LED’s usually provide a wider viewing angle without compromising image quality. Enjoy High-Quality Realistic Images with an LED Screen. Low Energy Consumption with an LED display. LED screens are Eco-Friendly, thanks to their reduced power consumption & longer lifespan.

Minimal Eye Strain with LED Screens- LEDs have virtually zero “warm-up” time (response time). A red indicator LED, for example, can achieve full brightness in less than a microsecond. The LEDs that are used in communications devices have an even faster response time. LED is cheaper than Television.

LED Displays are Easier and Cheaper Maintenance. As they are dust-proof, waterproof, and anti-corrosion, even when outside in extremes of weather, there is much less maintenance. An LED Screen has Ultra-High Contrast Levels. -LEDs can reproduce a vast palette of colors that’s far superior to any other kind of technology.

LED is cheaper than Television. LED Panels are easily replaceable - If a LED panel is broken it can easily remove and still use the LED screen just by replacing the broken panel not at all like TV screen. It is very much flexible and easy to use.
